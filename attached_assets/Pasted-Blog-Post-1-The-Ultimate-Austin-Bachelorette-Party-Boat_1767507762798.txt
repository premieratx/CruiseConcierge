You can party with your whole squad without worrying about losing anyone in a crowd. It’s private and personalized for your group (or multi-group if you join the ATX Disco Cruise, which we’ll explain shortly).

Something For Everyone: A Lake Travis boat party offers a mix of activities. The adventurous ones can jump into the water, float on lily pads, or ride a giant unicorn float. Others can sunbathe on deck or relax in the shade with a drink. There’s a dance floor for the party animals and plenty of chill space for those who want to chat and enjoy the views. No one will feel left out!

Stunning Scenery & Photos: The backdrop to your party will be clear blue waters, dramatic cliffs, and possibly a Texas sunset. It’s Instagram heaven for those insta-worthy bachelorette shots. Plus, with a professional photographer included on Premier Party Cruises (more on that below), you’ll capture every moment of the fun without needing your phone.

All-Inclusive Convenience: The top reason to choose a Lake Travis boat party – especially with a company like Premier Party Cruises – is how easy and stress-free it makes the celebration. Everything is included (except the booze, which you can BYOB). From a professional DJ pumping up the jams to coolers of ice for your drinks, your boat comes ready to party. You basically just show up and get down!

In short, a bachelorette party on Lake Travis combines the thrill of a nightclub, the relaxation of a pool party, and the bonding of a private getaway – all into one epic experience. It’s no wonder Austin bachelorette groups consistently rate a Lake Travis boat day as the highlight of their weekend.

ATX Disco Cruise vs. Private Charter: Finding Your Perfect Boat Bash

When planning your lake adventure, you have a couple of options. Premier Party Cruises offers both private boat rentals and their signature ATX Disco Cruise. Both are fantastic, but which is right for your group? Let’s break it down:

The World-Famous ATX Disco Cruise

If you’ve heard any buzz about Lake Travis bachelorette parties, you’ve probably come across the ATX Disco Cruise. This is an Austin-original shared party cruise exclusively for bachelorette and bachelor groups. Every Friday and Saturday, multiple small groups (like yours) come together on one big party barge to celebrate. Here’s why the ATX Disco Cruise is legendary:

It’s all-inclusive and hassle-free: As soon as you step aboard, the party is fully set up. The DJ is spinning your favorite dance hits, a professional photographer is ready to capture candids and group pics, huge floats are out, and all the party supplies (cups, coolers with ice, water, name tags, etc.) are provided. You don’t have to plan anything – just bring your drinks!

Multi-group = multi-fun: You’ll join other bachelorette (and bachelor) parties from around the country. It’s like a mashup party where everyone is in the celebratory mood. The energy is unbelievable – 50+ people dancing, laughing, and partying together. It’s an amazing feeling to have a big crowd, and many brides say they made new friends on the cruise. The multi-group atmosphere actually amps up the fun compared to a smaller private gathering.

Great for smaller groups: If you have, say, 5–15 girls, joining the ATX Disco Cruise is perfect because you get the big party experience without needing a huge group of your own. Even with a larger squad, you can all still join and be the life of the boat.

Cost-effective: It often costs less per person than renting a private boat (especially if your group is under 12). The ATX Disco Cruise packages range from about $85–$105 per person, which covers the boat, crew, DJ, photographer, equipment, and all amenities. To put in perspective, trying to replicate that on your own (renting a boat, hiring DJ & photographer, buying floats, etc.) could run over $3,000 total. With the Disco Cruise, you pay one flat per-person price and get incredible value. Plus, the bride often rides free on certain packages (like the Disco Queen or Super Sparkle options) if you have a big enough group!

What’s Included in ATX Disco Cruise: Every cruise ticket includes a 4-hour tour on Lake Travis, DJ, photographer (and free digital photos after), a 25-foot unicorn float, giant lily pad mats, BYOB with private coolers & ice, cups, koozies, even mimosa mixers (just bring champagne) – basically everything except your beverages. Did we mention the on-board disco dance floor and 14 disco balls? It’s a full-on themed party! And if the weather ever doesn’t cooperate, they have a Lemonade Disco backup plan (an indoor party) so it never rains on your parade.

So which to choose? If your bachelorette group is social and likes meeting others, or if you want a premium experience at a lower cost, the ATX Disco Cruise is a no-brainer. It’s Austin’s most famous bachelorette boat party for a reason. However, if your priority is a private, tailor-made day on the lake or you have scheduling constraints, a private bachelorette boat might be the better fit. Some savvy groups even do both: a private cruise one day for just the bride squad, and the Disco Cruise the next day to mingle and go crazy with new friends! Either way, Premier has you covered as the Lake Travis party boat experts.

FAQs: Austin Bachelorette Boat Parties

Q: How many people can we have on the boat?
A: Premier Party Cruises has boats that fit anywhere from 10 up to 75 people. The ATX Disco Cruise will typically have 50-100 people total, split among several bachelorette and bachelor groups. If you rent a private charter, you’ll choose a boat size for your group. For example, they have a 14-passenger boat, a mid-size 25-passenger, and a large party barge for 50+ guests. Most bachelorette groups are around 8-15 people, which is perfect for the Disco Cruise or a private boat. Always check with the company for capacity if you have a very large party.

Q: What do we need to bring on board?
A: Bring your drinks (and ID if those drinks are alcoholic!). All alcohol must be in cans or plastic – no glass on the boat for safety. Bring sunscreen, towels, and wear swimwear with party outfits that fit your theme (floppy hats, matching bikinis, “Bride Squad” tanks, etc.). It’s smart to bring some snacks and water as well – though water and ice are provided, you’ll want extra to stay hydrated. Don’t worry about cups, ice, coolers, speakers, floats – all that is included by Premier. And bring your party spirit! Props and costumes are welcome (just secure them so they don’t blow away).

Q: Is it safe to party on a boat?
A: Absolutely – safety is a top priority for Premier Party Cruises. They have a perfect safety record in 14+ years of operation. All captains are Coast Guard licensed and experienced. There are life vests on board for everyone. They also enforce rules to keep things fun but orderly – for example, they won’t allow anyone who is overly intoxicated to do risky things, and they have staff keeping an eye out while people swim. If someone in another group were to behave inappropriately, the crew deals with it (though bachelorette groups are usually too busy having fun to cause trouble!). You can feel at ease knowing you’re in good hands. Of course, follow the crew’s instructions, use common sense (no jumping off the top deck or anything crazy), and you’ll have a blast safely.

Q: What about bad weather?
A: Austin is sunny most of the time, but if truly bad weather hits, Premier has a Lemonade Disco backup plan – essentially they host an indoor party for everyone with a DJ and even include a meal/drinks if the boat can’t run. They very rarely have to invoke this (only a couple times in years), and they don’t cancel for just a light rain. The boats have covered areas, and a little warm rain can actually amp up the silliness (dancing in the rain, anyone?). Rest assured, your party will happen rain or shine – or move indoors for safety if needed.

Q: Can we get a refund if plans change?
A: Premier offers a 48-hour grace period after booking where you can get a full refund if you change your mind. This is great if you need to confirm with the group. Closer to the date, cancellations might not be refundable (since spots are limited), but they do allow you to add a couple extra people later if your group grows, space permitting. Travel insurance or booking with a credit card that has trip protection could be useful if you’re worried. But honestly, once you see how excited everyone is for the boat, cancelling will be the last thing on your mind!
