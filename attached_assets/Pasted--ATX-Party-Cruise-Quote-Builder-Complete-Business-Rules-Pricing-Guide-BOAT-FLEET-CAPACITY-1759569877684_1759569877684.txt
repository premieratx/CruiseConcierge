## DEPOSIT & PAYMENT RULES

### Deposit Requirements Based on Booking Timeline

**14+ Days Before Event:**
- Deposit: 25% of total
- Remaining balance due: 14 days before event date

**Less Than 14 Days Before Event:**
- Deposit: 50% of total
- Remaining balance due: 3 days after booking

### Payment Methods
- All payments processed through Stripe
- Credit/Debit cards accepted
- Deposit secures the booking
- Promo codes can be applied at checkout

## BOOKING FLOW LOGIC

1. **Party Type Selection:** User selects Bachelorette/Bachelor Party or Other Event
2. **Guest Count:** User enters number of guests
3. **Date Selection:** User picks event date
4. **Lead Capture:** Name, email, phone collected (creates lead in system)
5. **Cruise Selection Display:**
   - If Bach Party + Weekend + Disco Season → Show both Disco and Private options side-by-side
   - If weekday or off-season → Show Private Cruise options only
   - Display real-time availability for selected date
6. **Time Slot Selection:** User chooses from available time slots
7. **Package Selection:** User selects package tier
8. **Checkout:** Stripe payment processing with deposit calculation
9. **Confirmation:** Booking created, time slot capacity updated, notifications sent

---

## KEY BUSINESS RULES

1. **Time slots are LIVE** - once booked, capacity decreases in real-time
2. **Private cruises book the ENTIRE boat** - capacity decreases to 0 when booked
3. **Disco cruises are per-person** - capacity decreases by ticket count
4. **Conflicting time slots auto-blocked** - 30-minute buffer before/after private bookings
5. **Abandoned bookings tracked** - System logs incomplete checkout attempts for follow-up
6. **All times in Central Time (CST/CDT)** - Automatic DST handling
7. **Saturday is premium pricing** - Significantly higher rates reflect peak demand
8. **Disco cruises are Bach Party focus** - Marketing emphasizes bachelorette/bachelor groups

## CALCULATION EXAMPLES

**Example 1: Private Cruise**
- Date: Saturday
- Guests: 20
- Duration: 4 hours
- Package: Standard Essentials

Calculation:
- Base Rate: $375/hour × 4 hours = $1,500
