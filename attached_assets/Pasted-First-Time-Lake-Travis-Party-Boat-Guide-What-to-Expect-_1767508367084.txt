Step 2: Arrival and Boarding (What Actually Happens)

Most Lake Travis Party Boat experiences depart from marinas about 30–40 minutes outside downtown Austin.

Here’s what arrival looks like:

You arrive 20–30 minutes early

Group checks in together

Waivers are completed

Crew gives a quick safety briefing

You board the boat

There’s no confusion, no wandering, and no rush if you arrive on time.

For first-timers, this structured boarding process is reassuring—and sets the tone for a smooth experience.
