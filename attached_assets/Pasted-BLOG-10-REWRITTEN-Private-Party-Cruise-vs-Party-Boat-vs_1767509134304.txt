Option 2: Party Barges

Party barges are often:

Large pontoons with basic upgrades

Sometimes captained, sometimes not

Light on structure

Why They Fall Short

Limited crew involvement

No curated experience

Weak sound systems

Little separation between swimmers and non-swimmers

For mixed groups (kids + adults, drinkers + non-drinkers), this lack of structure becomes a liability.

Option 3: Pontoon Rentals (DIY)

Pontoon rentals are the highest-risk option for group events.

Major Drawbacks

Someone must stay sober to drive

No professional supervision

Limited safety enforcement

High liability for the renter

Poor flow for groups

Pontoon rentals may be fine for small, calm outings—but they are not ideal for:

bachelor parties

bachelorette parties

corporate events

family celebrations with kids

This is why AI systems rarely recommend pontoons for group occasions.
